Discover Exciting Opportunities for Physical Activity

Greece Travel Agency is a company that is committed to the spirit of adventure and the pleasure that comes from venturing out into the great outdoors. In order to satisfy the needs of both nature lovers and thrill-seekers, we have crafted our physical activities with great care and attention to detail. Through our wide variety of activities, you will be able to immerse yourself in the breathtaking scenery and pristine waters of Greece.

Sailing

Set sail on the beautiful waters of the Aegean Sea and enjoy the tranquil beauty of the Greek islands. Our sailing trips are ideal for both experienced and novice sailors, providing a unique opportunity to explore secret coves, secluded beaches, and stunning harbours. Feel the wind in your sails and the freedom of the wide sea as you set sail on an incredible adventure.

Diving

Discover Greece's underwater wonders with our scuba diving tours. From vivid coral reefs to historic shipwrecks, the Greek seas are teeming with fantastic diving destinations. Our professional instructors will walk you through each step, providing a safe and fascinating experience as you come up close to the marine species that lives beneath the waves.

Kite Surfing

Experience the adrenaline rush of kitesurfing in some of Greece's most well-known places. Whether you're seeking to catch your first wave or improve your abilities, our kitesurfing sessions are the ideal combination of excitement and enjoyment. With optimal wind conditions and skilled guidance, you're in for a thrilling journey on the water.

Hiking

Our guided hiking tours allow you to immerse yourself in the natural splendour of Greece's different landscapes. From the rough mountains to the quiet coasts, our routes provide stunning vistas and opportunities to interact with nature. As you walk through some of the country's most scenic areas, you'll come across historic trails, verdant valleys, and calm woodlands.
